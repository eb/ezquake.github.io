r_tracker_inconsole
Description:
Controls how tracker messages will appear in the console/notify area
Type:
enum
Remarks:
To see full death messages in the console, use con_fragmessages 2
ValueDescription
0Tracker messages will not be shown in the console
1Tracker messages will be shown in console and notify area (disables regular tracker messages)
2Tracker messages will be shown in console and notify area (regular tracker messages enabled)
3Tracker messages will be shown in console and regular tracker area but not in notify area
